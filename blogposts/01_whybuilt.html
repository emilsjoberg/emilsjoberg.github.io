Why I built this site

May 2013

I moved to San Francisco after college. I have had a few jobs and worked on many computers. On these computers and for these jobs I have been blessed to learn a lot. SQL and VBA were natural starting points. I went through a big Processing phase. I now find myself using Python and D3/JavaScript as my main tools. From those learnings, I output a lot of work; some rubbish and some I was super proud of. From the aforementioned work and computer transitions, a lot of my past output has been lost along the way. I thought it would be fun to build a central place for my work and thoughts, and so this site was born. Solving problems is why we code. I will see how effective this solution is. I will use this as a tool and reference it in the future. I hope others can as well.
